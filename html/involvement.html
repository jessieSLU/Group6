Purpose

As we all know Baguio is one of the most famous places here in the Philippines many people visit baguio or sometimes decides to live here in the summer capital of the phillipines but having many visitors and people deciding to stay here in our beautiful city also has its good and bad effects. As well know baguio is just a small peaceful city with many pine trees and cold weather and often visited by different people around the globe sometimes having to many people causes polution specilally the things that people use during travels like , cars, garbage,and many more. This group is so much aware on the effects of pollution in our environment that is why we would like to be involved in different eco-friendly, socioeconomic activities and to be an advocator of environmental protection.
Our group can provide necessary information to everybody on how it is dangerous to our health and how to prevent it in the least possible way we can do.

Reason Why we do this:

We want to save Baguio preserved it as much as possible.
-We want people to be aware what is happening to our Environment.
-We want them to realize that trees are important part of the environment.
-We want that as early as now that the youth will be aware and will continue to save our environment through our website.
-Lessen Pollution to our beloved city.
-Improved it to a more eco-friendly environment for us and the future generations
